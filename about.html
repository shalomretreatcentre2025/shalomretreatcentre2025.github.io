About The Shalom Retreat Centre

A sacred sanctuary for spiritual renewal and deeper connection with God

Our Mission

The Shalom Retreat Centre exists to provide a peaceful haven where individuals can step away from the busyness of daily life and encounter God's presence in a deeper, more meaningful way. We believe that in the stillness and beauty of creation, hearts are opened, spirits are renewed, and lives are transformed.

Our name, "Shalom," embodies the Hebrew concept of wholeness, peace, and divine harmony. This is not merely the absence of conflict, but the presence of completeness in body, mind, and spirit—a restoration to the way God intended us to live.

Our Vision

We envision a community of seekers—those longing for authentic spiritual encounters, healing from life's wounds, and clarity in their journey of faith. Through contemplative practices, guided spiritual direction, and the healing power of nature, we facilitate experiences that draw people closer to the heart of God.

Whether you come for a silent retreat, a weekend of spiritual renewal, or a day of contemplative prayer, our hope is that you will leave refreshed, restored, and more deeply rooted in God's love.

Our Core Values

Sacred Space

We honor the holiness of this place and create an atmosphere where God's presence can be encountered.

Compassion

We welcome all who seek rest and renewal with open hearts and gentle spirits.

Authenticity

We create safe spaces for honest wrestling with faith, doubt, and life's deepest questions.

Transformation

We believe in the life-changing power of encountering God and provide tools for lasting change.
